Golden Chicken

Description

This chicken dish combines elements from three of my favorite chicken recipes: chicken curry, chicken tagine, and a Creole-style smothered chicken.

The braising liquid is rich and delicious, getting its yellow golden color from turmeric and saffron and its intense flavor from a low-and-slow simmer.

Serve over buttery mashed potatoes, rice, or noodles. Ladle the sauce over top, and garnish with sliced green onions and minced jalapeños.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken leg quarters
  • 2 tsp salt + more to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • ½ cup diced jalapeño pepper
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp ground coriander
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp freshly ground blacke pepper
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ⅛tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 2½ cups cold water
  • 1 tsp saffron threads
  • 1 tsp dried currants
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon base
